Short Bio

Hello! I'm Dr. Iryna Malacina (Mah-LAH-see-nah), a post-doctoral researcher at the Business School of LUT University in Finland. I earned my D.Sc. in Supply Management from LUT.

My primary research focus is on supply network analysis, a field about which I am deeply passionate. However, my intellectual curiosity extends beyond that. I have also explored a range of topics including innovation management, environmental sustainability, and public procurement.

When it comes to methodology, you could say I'm a data enthusiast. I am skilled in a variety of analytical methods, particularly those related to network analysis. Currently, I am delving deep into Exponential Random Graph Models, but my toolkit also includes topic modeling, surveys, case studies, and literature reviews. Regardless of the method, I consistently bring a keen analytical eye to my work, which I consider to be one of my strengths.

Feel free to connect with me on social media or via email. I am always open to discussing new research ideas, exploring collaborative opportunities, or simply having engaging conversations about network analysis and supply management.
